The Aptean CRM provides outstanding customer service, by delivering a full-scale customer service and help desk management system. It enables representatives and the customer service team to track customer questions, problems, returns, and other issues.

Additionally, Aptean CRM features a knowledge base that provides instant access to ‘support professionals’, allowing them to quickly resolve cases and problems, regardless of complexity.

The key record types used to manage these processes are **Cases**, **Change Requests**, and **Knowledge**.
