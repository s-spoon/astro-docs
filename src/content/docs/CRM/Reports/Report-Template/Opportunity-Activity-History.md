Utilize the **Opportunity Activity History** report to generate a report with opportunity activity, including the following information:  
* **Activity Date** 
* **Action** 
* **Representative** 
* **Contact**
* **Company** 
* **Result**
* **Notes** 

The results are grouped by opportunity.
