Utilize the **Opportunity Detail** report to generate a report containing the following opportunity details: 
* **Method**
* **Representative** 
* **Amount** 
* **Date Open** 
* **Status**  
* **Priority** 
* **Source** 
* **Budget**
* **Expected Close**
