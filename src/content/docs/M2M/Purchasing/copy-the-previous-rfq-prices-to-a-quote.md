title: "Copy the previous RFQ prices to a Quote"

---

This method helps you locate RFQs by Part Number, Rev, Fac, and populate the Unit Price and RFQ number on the **Items** tab of a **Quote**.

**To copy the previous RFQ prices to a quote**

1. To copy the RFQ price and RFQ Number from the **History Screen (HIST)** page (for example, **Part Number:** **AF-334**), see **History Type- RFQ Summary(#)**.

2. To add a **Part No** to the quotation, either create a quote from scratch and add a part number through the **Items** tab.

    Or

    - Open the **Quotes (QUOTE)** page.

      1. Select **Sales Management** > **Sales Transactions** > **Quotes**.

            Or

      2. Search for **QUOTE** or **Quotes** in the **Navigation box** and then select **Quotes**.

           The **Quotes (QUOTE)** page appears.

3. Click the **Items** tab.

4. Click the **New** from the child toolbar.

    If the selected part exists in Quote then make the necessary changes.

5. Click the **Part Number** lookup reference.

    The **Lookup For Part Number** screen appears.

6. Select the same part number that you have chosen in the **History Screen (HIST)** page. For example, **Part Number: AF-334**.

    Highlight a row and click **Select**.

    Click **Cancel** to exit from the **Lookup For Part Number** screen. This returns to the **Quotes (QUOTE)** screen.

    The values are populated in the respective fields.

7. Click **Save** on the **Toolbar** and return to the **Sales Order (SO)** page.

8. Click the **Copy RFQ** on the grid toolbar.

9. Select the RFQ Number from the window.

    **Unit Price** and **RFQ** Number fields along with the remaining fields gets updated. This is picked from the previously selected **Request For Quote (RFQ)** price from the **History Screen (HIST)** screen.

10. Click **Save**.

    The selected **Part No** gets added and displayed in the **Items** grid.

    Click **Cancel** to discard the changes.
