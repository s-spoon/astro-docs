You can enter the estimated labor hours or a margin percentage or amount by entering the expected costs for each listed expense.

The system fills in the **Bill Customer for**: check boxes based on the active warranty for the machine associated with this service order when the service order is created.

## To estimate a service order's costs

1.  Open the **Field Service Control (FSORD)** page.

    1.  **Select Customer** **Relationship Management** > **CRM Transactions** > **Field Service**> **Field Service Control**.

    Or

    2.  *Search for* **FSORD** *or* **Field Service Control** *in the* **Navigation box** *and then select* **Field Service Control**.

       The **Field Service Control (FSORD)** *page appears.*

2.  On the **Toolbar**, click **More Actions**.

3.  Select **More Actions** > **Actions** > **Service Order Estimated Costs**.

    The **Service Order Estimated Costs (FSEST)** page appears.

4.  Type the estimated time that is expected for each type of service labor in the **Labor Hours** boxes.

5.  If a warranty is not in effect and you want to bill the customer for one or more of these types of labor, click the appropriate check boxes in the **Bill Customer for:** area.

    The labor rate appears in the **Labor Rate** boxes based upon the billing rate of the assigned field-service technician.

6.  To estimate a margin, type its percentage in the **+ Additional Margin @ (%)**

    box.

    Or, enter the amount in the **Of Sub-Total =** box.

    If you enter a percentage, the system uses the estimated subtotals to calculate the margin. If you enter an amount, the system calculates the percentage margin rate.

7.  On the **Toolbar**, click **Save**.

    You can create expenses for individual technicians for travel and lodging.
