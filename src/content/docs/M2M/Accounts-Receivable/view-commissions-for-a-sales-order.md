---
title: "View commissions for a sales order line item"
draft: false
type: Article
---

All sales order line items and line item releases can have specific commissions associated with them. You can view these commissions from the **Sales Order** form.

To view commissions for a sales order line item or line item release

1.  Open the **Sales Orders (SO)** page.

Select Sales Management > Sales Transactions > Sales Orders.

Or

1.  *Search for* **SO** *or* **Sales Orders** *in the* **Navigation box** *and then select* **Sales Orders**.

    **Sales Orders (SO)** *page appears.*

2.  On the **Toolbar**, click **Browse**.

    The **Browse For Sales Orders** window appears.

3.  Select the required row and click **Select**. Or, double-click the sales order whose commissions you want to view.
4.  Click the **Items** tab.
5.  Open the item for which you want to view the commission.
6.  Click the ellipsis under the **Action** column.
7.  Select **Grid Actions** > **Commissions**.

    The **Commissions (SORCOM)** window appears.

    Or to view the commission associated with a sales order release of this item: Select **Grid Actions** > **Sales Order Releases**.

    The **Sales Order Releases** window appears.

    **Sales Order Releases** option is enabled only for Multi-Release items.
