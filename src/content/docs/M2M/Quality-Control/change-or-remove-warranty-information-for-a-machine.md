You can change or remove warranty information for a machine provided there are no open service orders that are using that warranty and the warranty status is not active.

## To change or remove warranty information for a machine

1.  Open the **Machine (MACHSER)** page.

    1. Select **Customer Relationship Management** > **Master Data** > **Field Service** > **Machine**.

        Or

    1.  Search for **MACHSER** or **Machine** in the **Navigation box** and then select Machine. <br> The **Machine (MACHSER)** page appears.

1.  On the **Toolbar**, click **More Actions**.
2.  Select **More Actions** > **Actions** > **Machine Warranty**.

    The **Machine / Component Warranty (FSWAR)** screen opens.

3.  Select the machine:
    1.  On the **Toolbar**, click **Browse**.  <br> The **Browse For Machine** window appears.

    1.  Highlight a row and click **Select** or double-click the machine whose warranty information you want to change or remove.
2.  To change warranty information for a machine:
    1.  Make your changes.
    1.  Click **Save** on the **Toolbar**.
2.  To remove warranty information for a machine:
    1.  Click **Delete** on the **Toolbar**.

        A message, *Preparing to delete this information. Are you sure?* appears.

    1.  Click **OK** to confirm removal. Click **Cancel** to retain the value.
